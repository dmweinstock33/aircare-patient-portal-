Frequently Asked Questions

Does my insurance cover tracheostomy supplies?

Most insurance plans, including Medicare, Medicaid, and private insurance, cover tracheostomy supplies as durable medical equipment (DME). We'll verify your coverage for free before you enroll.

How often can I receive supplies?

Most insurance plans allow monthly shipments of tracheostomy supplies. We'll work with your insurance to ensure you receive the maximum allowable quantities on a regular schedule.

Do I need a prescription?

Yes, a prescription from your physician is required. Don't worry - we can coordinate with your doctor's office to obtain the necessary documentation.

How does the text reorder system work?

Each month, we'll send you a text message asking if you need your supplies refilled. Simply reply "YES" to confirm, and we'll process and ship your order. No phone calls or online logins required.

What if I need to change my order?

No problem. You can call us at 800-237-3699 or reply to any text message with your request. We're here to help adjust quantities, change products, or update your delivery schedule.

Is there any cost to me?

If your insurance covers tracheostomy supplies, your out-of-pocket cost depends on your plan's deductible and copay structure. Many patients receive supplies at little to no cost. We'll explain your specific costs before shipping.
